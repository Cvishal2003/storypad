# Product Roadmap

## Most Popular Features

Users love StoryPad for:

1. **Minimalist design** – Clean, distraction-free interface
2. **Rich editing toolbar** – Extensive formatting options without complexity

## Upcoming Features

### In Development

- **Routine** – Habit tracking and daily routine management
- **Inspiration to Template** – Convert inspirational content to templates (add-on enhancement)
- **Voice Journaling** – Record audio entries
- **Short Video** – Video recording support
- **Period Cycle Tracker** – New add-on for menstrual cycle tracking
- **Tablet/iPad UI** – Dedicated interface for larger screens

### Community Requests

**Most Requested:**

- Video recording ✓ (planned as "Short Video")
